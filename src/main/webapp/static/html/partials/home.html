Welcome to EventStore, the Event sharing and planning platform !

What is it ?

EventStore is a simple platform and app to help you plan events. Let's say you want to plan a secret birthday party for a friend. At first, you have to choose a date, a place, share ideas with potential attendees, to reply to theses questions. There EventStore helps you. You can design cooperatively events through simple steps.

Propose, decide, tease, enjoy !
